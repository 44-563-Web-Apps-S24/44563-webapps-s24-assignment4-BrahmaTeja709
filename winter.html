LOPAKHIN.

[Sighs heavily]

The sale ended up at four o'clock.... We missed the train, and had to wait till half-past nine. Ooh! My head's going round a little.

TROFIMOV.

[Moved]

My sun! My spring!

LOPAKHIN.

[In the doorway, calling after them]

Please, I ask you most humbly! Just a little glass to say good-bye.
